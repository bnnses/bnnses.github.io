Ask Us Anything
The Lumière is crafted exclusively from Grade 6A Mulberry Silk — the highest grade commercially available. It is naturally hypoallergenic, breathable, and possesses an incomparable lustre that lower grades simply cannot replicate.
We recommend hand-washing in cool water with a gentle silk-safe detergent, or dry-cleaning. Lay flat to dry — never wring or tumble dry. Iron on the lowest heat setting with a pressing cloth. With proper care, your Lumière will outlast every other piece in your wardrobe.
Yes — every order ships worldwide, free of charge, via tracked DHL Express. Delivery typically takes 3–5 business days to most locations. Signature gift packaging is included at no extra cost.
We offer a 30-day, no-questions-asked return policy on all unworn, unaltered pieces in original packaging. Simply contact us at hello@bnnses.com and we'll arrange free return shipping.
We offer complimentary blind-embossed initials on the gift box with any order. For bespoke commissions — custom colourways, monogramming on the silk itself, or corporate gifting — please reach out via our contact form below.
Intentional scarcity is part of our philosophy. The artisan dyeing process for each colourway is a small-batch operation — no two dye lots are perfectly identical. Keeping runs small preserves the integrity of each colour and ensures that wearing one is a genuinely rare experience.
